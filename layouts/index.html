about

Hi, my name is Hugues Huygens, I'm a freelance software engineer specialised in web development with a strong focus on user experience and user interface design.

After working a little more than 15 years as an employee under the wings of a big American company the time had come to spread my wings and fly!

So I started my own business in software development, user experience and user interface design.

Why 'untyped'?

My name is not easy to remember nor to write, so I wanted to find a different name for my business.

The term is derived from the lambda calculus theory, which is 'untyped' in it's purest form.

People tend to label everything... and I like to think I'm 'untyped'. (I think) A good software engineer should have a broad interest and should be eager to learn something new everyday. He should not stick to one programming language or framework, but choose the right tool for the job.
